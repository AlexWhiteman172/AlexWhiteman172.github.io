TOP 10 CORNVILLE FACTS; facts about the beatiful village that is cornville
1. Cornville's Corn Festival: Cornville hosts an annual Corn Festival where locals and visitors come together to celebrate everything corn-related. You can find corn-eating contests, corn mazes, and even a "Corniest Joke" competition.

2. Cornville's Giant Corn Statue: Cornville boasts a towering statue of an ear of corn that stands 30 feet tall. It's a popular landmark and a favorite spot for tourist photos.

3. Cornville's Unique Cornbread Recipe: Cornville is renowned for its unique cornbread recipe, a closely guarded secret passed down through generations. It's said to be the best cornbread you'll ever taste.

4. Cornucopia of Corn Products: Cornville is famous for its "Cornucopia Corner," a local shop that sells everything from corn-based snacks to corn-themed souvenirs.

5. Annual Corn Maze Challenge: Every fall, Cornville residents and tourists take on the challenge of navigating the town's famous corn maze, which covers a whopping 10 acres.

6. Cornville's Corny Parade: The town holds a whimsical parade featuring floats adorned with corn decorations. It's a colorful and fun event for all ages.

7. Cornville's Cornfield Symphony: During summer evenings, you can often hear the soothing sound of the wind rustling through the endless cornfields, creating a unique and peaceful "Cornfield Symphony."

8. Cornville's Cornhusking Competition: Cornhusking is a serious sport in Cornville. Locals compete annually to see who can husk the most corn in the shortest time.

9. Cornville's Annual Corn Art Festival: Artists from around the world flock to Cornville each year to participate in the Corn Art Festival, where they create stunning works of art using corn as their medium.

10. Cornville's Cornucopia Gardens: Cornville is home to magnificent gardens filled with various types of corn, including colorful varieties. These gardens are a sight to behold and a favorite spot for nature enthusiasts.
